5-Layer Scoring Pipeline

Each candidate flows through 5 intelligent scoring layers before final ranking

Layer 1+2

Career Fit Analysis

Title relevance, experience band (5-9yr), industry quality, production ML evidence in career descriptions. Penalizes consulting-only, title-chasers, and pure researchers.

Weight: 40%
Layer 3

Skills Intelligence

JD-specific must-have/nice-to-have matching with anti-keyword-stuffing. Cross-references endorsements, duration, and career descriptions to catch fake claims.

Weight: 30%
Layer 4

Behavioral Signals

Engagement (response rate, activity recency), availability (notice period, open-to-work), GitHub activity, interview reliability, and salary fit.

Weight: 20%
Layer 5

Honeypot Detection

Catches ~80 impossible profiles: impossible career dates, expert skills with zero evidence, behavioral contradictions. Rate >10% = disqualification.

Hard Filter
